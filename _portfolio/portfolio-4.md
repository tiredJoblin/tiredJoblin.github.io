This is a bullet hell adventure game with a mask switching gimmick that I made with a team in Tri Tech for the AMES gamejam.

Tools I used: Visual Studio Code, C#, Unity, and Github.

Skills I used: Teamwork, Leadership, Game Design, Creativity, Coding Knowledge, Creative Problem Solving.

What I learnt: How to lead a team, how to introduce a gimmick and force the player to use it, and how to use bools and integers to their fullest.

Challenges I solved: Getting the team to work more on the project during a moment of stagnation, and developing bullet patterns.
